About Coastwide Exterior Cleaning

Our Story

We're a family-run exterior cleaning business built on trust, hard work, and genuine care for the homes and properties in our community. Our business was founded on a simple idea — homes and businesses deserve to look their best without the hassle. From pressure washing to full exterior cleaning, we combine tough, hands-on work with professional standards to restore surfaces safely and effectively. Salt air, country grit, or years of buildup — we tackle it all with the right equipment, the right techniques, and a commitment to doing the job properly the first time. Fully insured, proudly local, and always focused on delivering a clean you can see and trust.

Meet the Owners

Coastwide Exterior Cleaning is proudly owned and operated by Matt and Nicole — a hardworking local couple who combine hands-on trade experience with genuine, professional customer service. Their business began with a simple goal: to build something of their own, work side by side, and provide a service they're truly proud of.

Photo

Matt

The Muscle of the Operation

Matt brings over 20 years of experience as a qualified plumber. After decades in the trade, he was ready for a fresh challenge that still allowed him to work with his hands and deliver high-quality results. As the muscle of the operation, Matt is out on the tools every day, handling pressure and soft washing with precision and care. Friendly, easy-going, and known for his playful nature, he takes pride in completing every job with attention to detail and absolutely no shortcuts.

Photo

Nicole

The Organised Force Behind the Scenes

With a background in real estate administration and customer service, Nicole understands the importance of presentation, communication, and property maintenance. Warm, community-minded, and genuinely invested in helping local families and businesses, Nicole brings a friendly, down-to-earth approach to every interaction. From accounts and bookings to quotes and customer communication, she ensures every client feels valued and supported from start to finish.

Together, Matt and Nicole have built Coastwide Exterior Cleaning on honesty, reliability, and genuine care for their customers. As a locally owned and operated business, they're proud to help homes and businesses across the region look their absolute best.
